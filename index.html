🧠 Key Pacing & Nutrition

🏊 Swim — 3.8 km (Target: 1:15:00)

  • Target pace: 1:58/100m
  • Steady effort; sight and settle early

🚴 Bike — 180 km (Target: 5:30–5:40)

  • Normalized Power: 190W (0.68 IF, 280W FTP)
  • Nutrition: 750ml fluids/hour (6 bottles), 75g carbs/hour (14–16 gels)
  • Reload bottles at Aslockton (special needs, each lap)
  • On-course nutrition available at Eden Hall/Aslockton

🏃 Run — 42.2 km (Target: 3:45–4:00)

  • Start pace: 5:30–5:45/km, build to 5:15–5:30/km if feeling strong
  • Gel every 40–45 min, drink water/electrolyte at every station
  • Adjust pace after 30 km to finish strong

✅ Final Checklist

  • Registration docs and ID packed
  • Bike racked, kit and nutrition labeled and prepped
  • Special needs bag handed in
  • Garmin charged and race file loaded
  • Timing chip and number wristband ready
  • Rested and mentally prepared
